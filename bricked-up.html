🏗 How to Play — Bricked Up

🧱 Bricked Up build

This is a separate version of the game with a brick-and-construction theme and the experimental Gear system. Your save here is independent from the main game and is stored under its own key.

🏗 Drop cycle

Your crane bucket auto-lowers, scoops bricks, and hoists them up to sell. You don't pilot it — you upgrade it.

⚡ Boost

Tap the boost button (or press Space) for +200% speed and pickup rate. Tap again while active to add +1 second, up to a 10s cap. Cooldown starts after it ends.

🔧 Equipment

Spend cash to upgrade Cable Length, Winch Motor, Brick Bin, Site Survey, and Cost Estimator. Levels are permanent within a run, but reset on Promotion.

🛠 Gear Workshop (Bricked Up–only)

Spend 📐 blueprints to permanently install one of five pieces of gear. Gear survives every promotion and stacks across runs. Each piece visibly attaches to your bucket. See the intro screen or open the workshop for details.

🧰 In Bin & encounters

Bricks scooped this drop show in your Bin. Every encounter — bonuses (Brick Delivery, Bricklayer's Trance, Master Brickworks) and the Brick to the Head hazard — comes from the Brick Lottery.

🏗 Brick Lottery

The lottery in the corner auto-pulls every 15s. Triple matches: 🚛 Brick Delivery (rare/epic crate burst, ≥3 legendary picks each, 10s), 👷 Bricklayer's Trance (10× value & 10× XP, 15s), 📐 Master Brickworks (legendary picks at 10× value, 15s), 🏗 GET BRICKED (all three). 🚧 triple = Brick to the Head (no loot for 10s).

📦 Material crates

Crates spawn occasionally. Click to add to your tray (next to the cash). Open them whenever — multi-item rolls biased toward higher rarity.

👷 Promotion & blueprints

Hit a tier's level threshold to promote (Apprentice → Laborer → …). Promotion banks blueprints based on your run's lifetime earnings and resets level/cash/upgrades. Each blueprint is +0.5% brick value, forever. Push deeper before promoting to bank more — or spend them in the Gear Workshop instead.

🏆 Honors

Achievements unlock as you play but aren't free — open the Honors panel and click each one to claim its cash reward.

📖 Materials Catalog

Tracks every unique brick you've ever hauled. Empty slots are still buried somewhere.

🏗 Welcome to Bricked Up

This is a separate version of the game with a brick-and-construction theme and the experimental Gear system. Your save here is fully independent from the main game — you start fresh. The new feature: spend 📐 blueprints in the Gear Workshop to permanently outfit your crew. Five pieces of gear, each capping at Lv 10:

  • ⛑Brick-Lined Hardhat — Brick to the Head duration shortened.
  • 🔨Mason's Trowel — Positive lottery bonuses last longer.
  • 📐Brick Press — Each promotion stamps more blueprints.
  • 🍀Lucky Brick — Lottery odds shift toward GET BRICKED, away from inspectors.
  • 🧠Bricklayer's Insight — Bonus XP on every brick pickup and crate.

Trade-off: blueprints spent on gear no longer give their +0.5% brick value forever, so each level is a real long-term decision. Gear survives every promotion — only a full Reset wipes it. Equipped pieces are visible on your bucket.
