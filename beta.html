🤿 How to Play — Gear Beta

🧪 Gear Beta build

This is a separate version of Deep Sea Salvage with the experimental Gear system. Your save here is independent from the main game and is stored under its own key.

🌊 Dive cycle

Your sub auto-dives, collects loot, and surfaces to sell. You don't pilot it — you upgrade it.

⚡ Boost

Tap the boost button (or press Space) for +200% speed and pickup rate. Tap again while active to add +1 second, up to a 10s cap. Cooldown starts after it ends.

🔧 Outfitting

Spend cash to upgrade Hull, Thrusters, Cargo, Sonar, and Appraiser. Levels are permanent within a run, but reset on Promotion.

🛠 Gear Workshop (beta-only)

Spend 🔮 pearls to permanently install one of five pieces of gear. Gear survives every promotion and stacks across runs. Each piece visibly attaches to your sub. See the intro screen or open the workshop for details.

🎒 On Deck & encounters

Items collected this dive show in On Deck. Every encounter — bonuses (Chest Frenzy, Mermaid's Kiss, Treasure Map) and the Shark Attack hazard — comes from the Salvage Slot.

🎰 Salvage Slot

The slot in the corner auto-pulls every 15s. Triple matches: 📦 Chest Frenzy (rare/epic chest burst, ≥2 legendary picks each, 10s), 🧜 Mermaid's Kiss (3× value & 5× XP, 15s), 🗺 Treasure Map (legendary picks at 10× value, 15s), 🌟 Jackpot (all three). 🦈 triple = Shark Attack (no loot for 10s).

📦 Treasure chests

Chests spawn occasionally. Click to add to your tray (next to the cash). Open them whenever — multi-item rolls biased toward higher rarity.

⚓ Promotion & pearls

Hit a tier's level threshold to promote (Deckhand → Salvager → …). Promotion banks pearls based on your run's lifetime earnings and resets level/cash/upgrades. Each pearl is +0.5% loot value, forever. Push deeper before promoting to bank more — or spend them in the Gear Workshop instead.

🏆 Honors

Achievements unlock as you play but aren't free — open the Honors panel and click each one to claim its cash reward.

📖 Field Journal

Tracks every unique item you've ever salvaged. Empty slots are still out there.

🧪 Gear Beta

🛠 Welcome to the Gear Beta

This is a separate version of Deep Sea Salvage with the experimental Gear system. Your save here is fully independent from the main game — you start fresh. The new feature: spend 🔮 pearls in the Gear Workshop to permanently customize your sub. Five pieces of gear, each capping at Lv 10:

  • 🛡Reinforced Hull — Shark Attack duration shortened.
  • ✨Bonus Stabilizer — Positive slot bonuses last longer.
  • 🔮Pearl Compressor — Each promotion banks more pearls.
  • 🍀Lucky Salvage Charm — Slot odds shift toward jackpots, away from sharks.
  • 🧠Salvager's Insight — Bonus XP on every loot pickup and chest.

Trade-off: pearls spent on gear no longer give their +0.5% loot value forever, so each level is a real long-term decision. Gear survives every promotion — only a full Reset wipes it. Equipped pieces are visible on your sub.
