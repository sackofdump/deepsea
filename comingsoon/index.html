⛧ How to Play — Ascension

🕯 Ascension

A prototype with a dark-mystic descent theme and a wholly-different progression system. There is no gear shop. Each ascension hits a level cap that escalates (Lv 20, 30, 40, ...) — you MUST ascend to continue. Every ascension multiplies your Ascension Multiplier ×1.5 and banks +2 🪙 Coins for the Talent Vault.

🕯 Descent cycle

Your censer auto-descends through realms of bone and sigil, gathers relics, and returns to the altar to sell. You don't pilot it — you ascend it.

⚡ Boost

Tap boost (or press Space) for +200% speed and pickup rate. Tap again while active to add +1s, up to 10s.

⛧ Ascend

No shop. Ranks 2, 4, 6, 8, 10 auto-unlock permanent ×2, ×3, ×5, ×10, ×25 Vow multipliers — stacking to ×7,500 at the Lv 100 cap. Every ascension also multiplies your Ascension Multiplier ×1.5 (compounding, no cap) AND banks +2 🪙 Coins for the Talent Vault.

🪙 Talent Vault — permanent talents

Every Lv 1 → 100 run earns you 2 🪙 Coins. Spend them in the vault on 8 talents, each 3 ranks:

  • 🎰 Bone-Reader — slot match chance (+15 / +30 / +50%)
  • ✨ Sigil-Touched — pickup value (+20 / +45 / +80%)
  • 👁 Inner Sight — bonus XP (+20 / +45 / +80%)
  • 💀 Apotheotic — slot buff duration (+20 / +45 / +80%)
  • 🧲 Wraithgrasp — pickup rate (+50 / +100 / +200%)
  • 🌑 Black Communion — mega cascade hit chance (+5 / +10 / +20%)
  • 🕯 Vesper's Favor — loot rarity uplift (+15 / +30 / +50%)
  • 💎 Tribute — pickups duplicate (+5 / +12 / +25%)

Costs 1 / 2 / 3 coins per rank (6 coins to max one talent · 48 to max all 8 → about 24 ascensions to fully complete the tree at 2 coins per run). Coins are persistent forever — they're not wiped by ascending, only by a full reset.

⛧ Oracle Bones

The bones are read every 15s. Triple matches:

  • 🩸 Stigmata — pickups halt for 10s.
  • 🔔 Vespers Frenzy — relic-crate burst for 12s, ≥2 black-box reliquaries guaranteed.
  • 🕯 Communion — 3× value & 2× XP for 18s.
  • 💀 Apotheosis — forced legendary picks for 15s.
  • 🐍 Serpent's Coil — reliquary capacity ×2 for 12s.
  • 👁 Inner Eye — 3× XP for 12s (no value bonus).
  • 🌑 BLACK MASS — every positive bonus stacked. Progressive jackpot: after the main slot lands, 3 side mini-slots chain in. Each must land 3-of-3 🌑 (50% per cascade). All four must hit or you bank zero. All four = 40s MEGA.

📦 Reliquaries

Crates spawn occasionally. Click to add to your tray (next to the cash). Open them whenever — multi-item rolls bias toward higher rarity.

⛧ The Run Loop

First cap is Lv 20, each ascension's cap climbs by 10 up to Lv 100 (and stays there forever). Each ascension resets level/cash/upgrades but keeps: rank, Ascension Multiplier (×1.5 compounding), 🪙 Coins, talents, and Vow milestones. Vows unlock at ranks 2/4/6/8/10. Total power = Vows × Ascension Multiplier × Talents.
