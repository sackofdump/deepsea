🌷 How to Play — Spring Bloom

🌸 Limited time event

Spring Bloom has its own leaderboard. Final standings lock at midnight on May 8. Your save here is separate from the main game.

🌊 Trip cycle

Your forager auto-descends into the bloom, gathers spring loot, and surfaces to sell. You don't pilot it — you upgrade it.

⚡ Boost

Tap the boost button (or press Space) for +200% speed and pickup rate. Tap again while active to add +1 second, up to a 10s cap. Cooldown starts after it ends.

🔧 Outfitting

Spend cash to upgrade Hull, Thrusters, Basket, Sonar, and Appraiser. Levels are permanent within a run, but reset on Bloom Up.

🧺 In Basket & encounters

Items collected this trip show in your basket. Every encounter — bonuses (Chest Frenzy, Butterfly Kiss, Rainbow Pact) and the Spring Storm hazard — comes from the Bloom Slot.

🌷 Bloom Slot

The slot in the corner auto-pulls every 15s. Triple matches: 🪺 Chest Frenzy (rare/epic chest burst, ≥2 legendary picks each, 10s), 🦋 Butterfly Kiss (5× value & 10× XP, 15s), 🌈 Rainbow Pact (legendary picks at 10× value, 15s), 🌷 Bloom Burst (all three). 🌧 triple = Spring Storm (no loot for 10s).

📦 Treasure chests

Bronze/Silver/Gold chests spawn occasionally. Click to add to your tray (next to the cash). Open them whenever — multi-item rolls biased toward higher rarity.

🌱 Bloom Up & pollen

Hit a tier's level threshold to bloom up (Sprout → Bud → …). Bloom Up banks pollen based on your run's lifetime earnings and resets level/cash/upgrades. Each pollen is +0.5% loot value, forever. Push deeper before blooming up to bank more.

🏆 Honors

Achievements unlock as you play but aren't free — open the Honors panel and click each one to claim its cash reward.

🌸 Welcome to Spring Bloom

Limited-time event

Spring Bloom runs until midnight on May 8, 2026. Once the timer hits zero the leaderboard locks and the event closes — push as deep as you can before then.

Separate save

Your progress here is its own world — it doesn't touch your main Deep Sea Salvage save. Cash, level, pollen, codex, and honors all start fresh.

Bloom Slot bonuses

The slot in the corner pulls every 15 seconds. Triple matches trigger:

  • 🪺Chest Frenzy — a burst of treasure chests spawn rapidly for 10s, weighted toward rares and epics. Each frenzy chest you collect rolls at least 2 legendary picks when you open it.
  • 🦋Butterfly Kiss — 5× value and 10× XP on every pickup for 15s. Stacks with Appraiser and Pollen.
  • 🌈Rainbow Pact — every pickup forces the highest-rarity tier in the current biome for 15s. Forced legendaries also get a 25× value multiplier on top.
  • 🌷Bloom Burst (jackpot) — Chest Frenzy and Butterfly Kiss and Rainbow Pact, all at once. The big one.
  • 🌧Spring Storm (hazard) — no loot at all for 10s. Slot bonuses drain this 10× faster, so keep spinning.

Own leaderboard

The leaderboard on this page only shows Spring Bloom scores — separate ranking from the main game. Set a name in the leaderboard panel and click Sync to compete.
