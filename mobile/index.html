🤿 How to Play

🌊 Dive cycle

Your sub auto-dives, collects loot, and surfaces to sell. You don't pilot it — you upgrade it.

⚡ Boost

Tap the boost button for +200% speed and pickup rate. Tap again while active to add +1 second, up to a 10s cap. Cooldown starts after it ends.

🔧 Outfitting

Spend cash to upgrade Hull, Thrusters, Cargo, Sonar, and Appraiser. Levels reset on Promotion.

🎒 On Deck & encounters

Items collected this dive show in the Sub tab. Every encounter — bonuses (Lucky Current, Mermaid's Kiss, Treasure Map) and the Shark Attack hazard — comes from the Salvage Slot.

🎰 Salvage Slot

The slot in the corner auto-pulls every 15s. Triple matches: 🌊 Lucky Current (2× cargo, 15s), 🧜 Mermaid's Kiss (2× value, 15s), 🗺 Treasure Map (legendary picks, 15s), 🌟 Jackpot (all three, 30s). 🦈 triple = Shark Attack (speed cut for 5s).

📦 Treasure chests

Bronze/Silver/Gold chests spawn occasionally. Tap to add to your tray (next to the cash). Tap a tray chest to open — multi-item rolls biased toward higher rarity.

⚓ Promotion & pearls

Hit a tier's level threshold (every 10 levels) to promote. Promotion banks pearls based on your run's lifetime earnings and resets level/cash/upgrades. Each pearl is +0.5% loot value, forever.

🏆 Honors

Achievements unlock as you play but aren't free — open the Honors tab and tap each one to claim its cash reward.

📖 Field Journal

Tracks every unique item you've ever salvaged. Empty slots are still out there.
